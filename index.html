About Us

WELCOME TO KONECCC

Kingdom of New Creatures in Christ Center (KONECCC) is the church arm of GOPEM Ministry, committed to spiritual growth, discipleship, fellowship, and raising believers who impact their world with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

KONECCC is more than a church — we are a family rooted in full love, guided by faith, and driven by purpose. Our vision is to raise Bible addicts and excellent people who influence every sphere of life with kingdom principles.

We teach the undiluted truth of scriptures with Christ as the central focus, and we take discipleship seriously through regular meetings and seminars.

Our Mission

To raise disciples grounded in Christ, emphasizing the Word, prayer, excellence, and community — building believers who will transform their world through the power of the Gospel.
